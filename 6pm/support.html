Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change which apps are blocked?

Open 6pm, tap the gear icon in the top right corner, then tap "change blocked apps." There's a 30-second wait to prevent impulsive changes, then the app picker will open.

Why can't I open my blocked apps?

That's the point! 6pm uses Apple's Screen Time API for real, system-level blocking. Between 6 PM and 6 AM, your selected apps are completely inaccessible. They'll unlock automatically at 6 AM.

How do I turn off the live activity on my lock screen?

Open 6pm, tap the gear icon, then tap "live activity" to toggle it off.

Does 6pm collect any of my data?

No. Everything runs on your device. No accounts, no servers, no tracking. See our privacy policy for details.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com, find 6pm in your purchase history, and request a refund there.

The app isn't blocking anything

Make sure you granted Screen Time / Family Controls permission during setup. If you skipped app selection, open settings (gear icon) and choose which apps to block. If issues persist, try deleting and reinstalling the app.
