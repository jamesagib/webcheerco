Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

6pm ("the app") is developed by James Agib. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

6pm does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

What Stays on Your Device

Your app selection (which apps you chose to block) is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure app container. Your preferences (such as the live activity toggle) are stored locally on your device. No data is sent to any server, third party, or external service.

Screen Time & Family Controls

6pm uses Apple's Screen Time API (Family Controls framework) to block apps during the hours you choose. This is handled entirely by the operating system on your device. We do not have access to your usage data, screen time statistics, or any information about which apps you use or how often.

No Accounts

6pm does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

No Analytics or Tracking

6pm does not use any analytics frameworks, crash reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

No Third-Party Services

6pm does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

Children's Privacy

6pm does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page with the new date. Since we don't collect your email or any contact information, please check this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at jagib07@gmail.com.
