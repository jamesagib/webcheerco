🔒 Digital Spoons Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 21, 2026

Welcome to Digital Spoons. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • a. Information You Provide
    • Player name – used locally for gameplay display only
    • Game preferences and settings – stored locally on your device
  • b. Automatically Collected Information
    • Device identifier – used solely to enable local multiplayer connectivity
    • Game history and statistics – stored locally on your device
  • c. Local Network Data
    • Digital Spoons uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework to discover and connect with nearby players over Wi-Fi and Bluetooth
    • No data is transmitted over the internet – all gameplay communication happens directly between devices on the local network

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable local multiplayer gameplay between nearby devices
  • To display your chosen player name to other players during a game session
  • To store game history and statistics locally on your device
  • To improve app performance and stability

3. How We Share Your Information

  • We do not sell, share, or transmit your personal data to any third parties
  • Your player name is shared only with other players on the same local network during an active game session
  • No data is sent to external servers – Digital Spoons operates entirely locally

4. Data Storage

  • All data (player name, game history, settings) is stored locally on your device
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all stored data
  • We do not maintain any external databases or cloud storage of user data

5. Your Choices

  • You can change your player name at any time
  • You can revoke local network permissions in your device's Settings app
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app

6. Children's Privacy

Digital Spoons does not collect personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require an account or sign-up.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
