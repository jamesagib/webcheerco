Welcome to HydrateAI. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • a. Information You Provide
    • Your email or username (if you sign up)
    • Water intake logs, drink types, and hydration goals
    • Preferences such as reminders and notifications
  • b. Automatically Collected Information
    • Device information (model, OS, app version)
    • Usage data (when and how you use the app)
    • Crash reports or analytics (to improve the app)
  • c. Optional Integrations
    • If you connect Apple Health or Google Fit, we may access hydration-related data (with your explicit permission).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To track and display your hydration progress
  • To send personalized reminders and notifications
  • To improve our features and fix bugs
  • To analyze anonymized usage trends for product decisions

3. How We Share Your Information

  • We do not sell your data. We may share anonymized data:
    • With third-party analytics providers (e.g. Supabase)
    • If required by law (e.g. court orders)

4. Your Choices

  • You can delete your account and data at any time in the app settings
  • You can opt out of push notifications
  • You can revoke access to Apple Health / Google Fit

5. Data Retention

We retain your hydration data until you delete it or your account.

6. Children's Privacy

HydrateAI is not intended for users under 13. If you're under 13, please do not use the app.
